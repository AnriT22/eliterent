Insurance Information:

TPL – Third Party Liability:
Covers damage caused to other vehicles. Does not cover: your rented vehicle.

CDW – Collision Damage Waiver:
Covers damage to the rented vehicle in case of an accident. Does not cover: tires, glass, or interior damage.

Full Coverage:
Covers all damages, theft, and roadside assistance.
Includes: TPL + CDW + full protection.
