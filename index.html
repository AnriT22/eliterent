Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting a car in Georgia

Do I need an international driving license? +

No. Georgia accepts most foreign driving licenses. Visitors from EU, USA, UK, Israel, Russia, Turkey, and many other countries can drive with their domestic license for up to 1 year.

Is a deposit required? +

No deposit is required on most vehicles. We offer no-deposit car rentals from verified local partners across Georgia.

Can I pick up at Tbilisi Airport? +

Yes! We offer 24/7 airport pickup and drop-off at Tbilisi (TBS), Kutaisi (KUT), and Batumi (BUS) airports. Free delivery is available for most vehicles.

Can I drive to Kazbegi or Svaneti? +

Yes. Many of our SUVs and 4x4 vehicles are approved for mountain roads including Kazbegi, Mestia/Svaneti, Tusheti, and the Georgian Military Highway.

Is insurance included? +

Yes, basic insurance (TPL) is included with all rentals. You can also add CDW or Full Coverage for additional protection.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept cash (USD, EUR, GEL), credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment method availability varies by partner.

Can I cancel my booking for free? +

Yes! Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before your pickup time on most vehicles.
